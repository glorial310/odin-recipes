Margarita

A margarita is a cocktail that can be served shaken or blended with ice, or straight. The drink is traditionally served in a margarita glass, a stepped-diameter glass or champagne coupe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lime wedge
  • 1 pinch coarse sea salt to taste
  • 1 large ice cube
  • Ice cube, desired amount
  • 2 fluid ounces white tequila
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces triple sec
  • 1 fluid ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 slice of lime

Steps

  1. Moisten rim of glass with a lime wedge
  2. Sprinkle salt onto a plate, lightly dip moistened rim into the salt
  3. Place large ice cube into glass, set aside
  4. Add into cocktail shaker: tequila, triple sec, and lime juice
  5. Cover and shake vigorously until shaker is chilled
  6. Strain into prepared glass and garnish with slice of lime
  7. Serve
