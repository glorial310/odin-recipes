Mojito

Mojito is a traditional Cuban highball, popular in the summer. The cocktail consists of 5 ingredients. This drink is sweet, citrus, and herbaceous mint flavour to complement the rum.

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 medium lime, cut into 3 wedges
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 cup ice cubes, or as desired
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces white rum
  • 1/2 cup club soda, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedges into a glass, muddle and crush to release mint oils and lime juice
  2. Add remaining lime wedges and sugar, muddle again
  3. Fill glass with ice
  4. Pour rum and fill glass with club soda
  5. Stir, taste, and serve
