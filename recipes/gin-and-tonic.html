Gin and Tonic

Gin was born around 1689. Gin and tonic is a highball cocktail with gin and tonic water poured over a large amount of ice. The ratio of gin to tonic varies according to taste, strength of gin, other drink mixers being used, and preference.

Ingredients

  • 4 ice cubes
  • 2 fluid ounces gin
  • 4 fluid ounces tonic water
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 lime wedge

Steps

  1. Place ice cubes into a glass of choice
  2. Pour gin and tonic water over ice
  3. Pour lime juice over ice
  4. Stir
  5. Garnish with lime wedge
  6. Serve immediately
